# Specification: Header display on mobile

## Objective
Fix header on mobile so that everything fits in the screen width (no scrolling horizontally).

## Requirements
- The tags: if they do not fit, they should be compacted as drop-down menu.
- The buttons: if they do not fit, another drop-down menu.
- Alternative: Move to a new sidebar on the right.
- Add functionality to reveal or hide with swipe on mobile.
